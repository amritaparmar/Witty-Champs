FAQs

If all classes are not exhausted in the current month, will it be carried forward to next month?

Answer : No. You have to renew the subscription after every 30 days to get fresh class passes

Can I share my account or class passes with other kids?

Answer : Sharing accounts is a violation of WittyChamps' terms and conditions and will invite deactivation of account

Can I pause my subscription?

Answer : Subscription cannot be paused in between the month.

Can I take a break in between months and will my account be active in that case?

Answer : You can take a break in between months and resume your subscription any time.

Can I have the class pass model and also enrol for clubs?

Answer : Yes you may.

Can I share my child’s account or class passes with my other child?

Subscription is valid per child. Sharing of account or class passes is a violation of WittyChamps' terms and conditions and will invite deactivation of account

Is my account or balance class passes transferable to some other child?

Answer : No

FAQS

Can I get a trial class?

Answer : Trial class date will be published. You may enrol for the trial class. If you miss the date of trial class, recorded video of the trial will be available on the club page. You may contact Mob # 9611710006 for any further assistance on this

What if I am not able to find a club of my child’s interest?

Answer : You may make a suggestion by clicking on ‘Couldn’t find the course, make a suggestion’ tab and type in your request. We will try to launch that course, if possible at the earliest and keep you posted. You may also drop a message to Mob # 9611710006 for personal assistance on this.

What if I do not like the club and would like to cancel subscription?

Answer : Clubs with duration less than 10 days does not qualify for cancellation of memberships. If the club duration is more than 10 classes and you wish to cancel your club subscription for any reason, you may do so within completion of 2 classes of that club. 80% refund will be provided in this case. Incase the class proceeds beyond 2 classes then cancellation will not be possible.

Can I share my account or club passes with other kids?

Answer : Sharing accounts is a violation of WittyChamps' terms and conditions and will invite deactivation of account

Can I take a break in between the club duration and resume it when the next batch starts?

Answer : Yes, you may enrol your child again the next time the club (also called workshops) resumes. However, if the repetition does not happen then refund will not be done. You may stay in touch with our representative at mob # 9611710006 to understand when we will be launching the same workshop again.

Can I discontinue from one club and join another with the same fee submitted?

Answer : No, the club fee cannot be transferred to any other course / class.

Can I have the class pass model and also enrol for clubs?

Answer : Yes you may.

Can I share my child’s account or class passes with my other child?

Answer : Subscription is valid per child. Sharing of account or class passes is a violation of WittyChamps' terms and conditions and will invite deactivation of account

Is my account or club passes transferable to some other child?

Answer : No
